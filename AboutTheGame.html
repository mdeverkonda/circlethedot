The Circle the Dot Game


The Circle the Dot Game is an infuriating game that requires you to surround a moving dot with your stationary dots


It looks a little somthing like this


Every turn, you are able to place an orange dot. After you place, the blue dot is able to move into any adjacent circle.

Your job is to surround the dot before it leaves the map.
